# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

## Data Collection

MemeChat processes all data entirely on-device. No personal data, chat messages, images, or usage patterns are uploaded, collected, or transmitted to any remote server.

## On-Device Processing

All AI inference, embedding generation, vector search, and image analysis are performed locally on your device using on-device models. No internet connection is required for core functionality.

## Web Search

When you explicitly enable web search, MemeChat sends your query to a search engine to retrieve results. This is opt-in and occurs only when you activate the feature per conversation.

## Third-Party Services

MemeChat does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

## Data Storage

Conversation history and model files are stored exclusively on your device's internal storage. You can delete all data at any time through the app's Settings screen.

## Permissions

MemeChat requests only the permissions necessary for its core functionality, such as storage access for loading models and images you choose to share with the assistant.

## Changes

Any updates to this policy will be reflected in the app.
